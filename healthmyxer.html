Healthmyxer

Project Specifications

BRIEF:

The aim of this project was to create a mobile app to help people treat common symptoms and illnesses with ancient-medicinal holistic practices. The app was designed to take in your symptoms and provide diet and exercise based on holistic medicine practices, such as specific yoga poses or breathing exercises.

MY ROLE:

My role in this project was as a graphic designer. I was responsible for creating the app's logo and designing the figures for one of the key breathing exercises called Pranayama.

TOOLS:

The assets I created were made using Adobe Illustrator.

KEY TAKEAWAYS:

This was my first ever freelance project, and a real testing of my graphic design abilities. Switching from a UX/UI design role to a graphic design role was a challenge, but I was able to create a logo and figures that the client was very happy with. It also gave me the chance to further expand on my Adobe Illustrator skills by creating more intricate graphics than I would typically need for my UX/UI projects.
