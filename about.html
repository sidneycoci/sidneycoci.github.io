Education

University of Miami

I enrolled at the University of Miami as a freshman in the fall of 2022. I originally enrolled as a Computer Science major pursuing a Bachelor of Arts, but have since then added on an additional major in Interactive Media.

At the University of Miami, I am apart of numerous groups and clubs. I work as a User Experience designer at the XR Studio on campus, where I am currently working on an Exposure Therapy project in VR. As well as this, I am a web designer at UM's student-run consultancy, Orange Umbrella. I am also an active member of the Google Student Developers Club, and I occasionally do web design work for UMiami's student-run magaizne, Distraction.

Chopticon High School

I graduated from Chopticon High School in Morganza, Maryland in the spring of 2022. I graduated with high honors and was ranked fifth in my graduating class.

During my time at Chopticon, I was involved in a number of organizations and groups. Most notably, I was elected class historian for all four years, and in that position helped plan events including prom and graduation. I was in the National Honor Society as well, where I was elected as Historian for two years. I was a member of the soccer team for all four years, being on JV my freshman year and Varsity for the following three years. My largest accomplishment in high school was my AP Capstone achievement award, where I did a year long study about the perception of women's reproductive healthcare in Maryland compared to Texas.
